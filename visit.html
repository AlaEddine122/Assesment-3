Visit Boumerdes

Must see experiences!

Why should I go to Boumerdes?

Boumerdes is considered one of the most peaceful places in Algeria. People from all over the country travel to boumerdes in hopes to relax and break their daily routine. The nature and scenery are beautiful and it is a great place for one to release stress and forget about all of their problems for some time. Not to mention, the prices are extremely low, you could spend just under $100 in a week and eat extremely well and participate in all sorts of activities.

When is the best time to go?

Obviously, a place that is known for its beaches and nature is best to visit during the summer. However, you would be surprised at how beautiful Boumerdes is in the winter, and not to mention, the spring. It all depends on what you want to experience. If you are in the mood to get a tan and have a nice cold drink by the beach, GO IN THE SUMMER! If you want to see all sorts of colours on plants and feel a nice breeze whilst you stroll through the communtiy park, then the spring is the time for you.

The rotten rock (also known as rocher noir in french) is a monumental boulder located on the coast of one of many Boumerdes beaches. The rock is named rocher noir because Boumerdes' former name was also rocher noir. The rock is a common place for tourists to take photos and it provides a wonderful backdrop of nature as waves splash along the sides of the rock. It has stood tall for over 100 years and the people of Algeria hope for it to stay for much much longer.

Abdul Rahman bin Khaldoun mosque was opened in 2008. It is used daily by the muslim community in Boumerdes for the 5 daily prayers. It tells a story about how the community got together and raised money to build a mosque on behalf of a brother who had passed away. The architecture is of turkish inspiration and it is a beautiful sight to see regardless of if you are muslim or not.
