About Boumerdes

Boumerdes is a city on the North coast of algeria with an approximate population of 802,083 people in just a 1356 km squared area. Most of the population are young algerians pursuing degrees, opening businesses, or simply just living life and enjoying the breeze that comes from the coast of the Mediterranean Sea.

Student Life

With 3 universities, Boumerdes is considered home for over 80,000 students. Students study everything ranging from Business to Medicine.

Beaches

Boumerdes is known for it's beautiful beaches, where students and families ocasionally go to spend some time.

People

The people in Boumerdes are known for their generosity and friendliness. The majority of the population are muslims and they are very accepting and welcoming of everyone regardless of ethnicity or religion. They are all either working, studying, and oftentimes both.
