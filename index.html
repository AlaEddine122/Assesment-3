About Boumerdes

Boumerdes is a city on the North coast of algeria with an approximate population of 802,083 people in just a 1356 km squared area. Most of the population are young algerians pursuing degrees, opening businesses, or simply just living life and enjoying the breeze that comes from the coast of the Mediterranean Sea.
